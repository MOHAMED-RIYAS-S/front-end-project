About the Department of Artificial Intelligence And Data Science

The Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science was established to provide students with knowledge in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Analytics, Deep Learning, and related technologies. The department focuses on developing problem-solving skills, innovation, and research abilities to meet the growing demands of the IT industry. Students are trained through practical learning, projects, workshops, and industry-oriented activities to become successful professionals in the field of AI and Data Science.
